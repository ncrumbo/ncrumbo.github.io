About Us

We are a family owned tradional style pizzeria serving the community for 30 years! We are known for our light, airy and a hint of sweet pizza crust. We pride quality on our homemade pizza dough and sauce. Each pizza we make is hand-tossed with the best farm fresh high quality ingredients. We put you in the driver's seat and let you create the pizza to satisfy your cravings or you may order one of our specialities! So come visit us, we offer dine-in, take-out and delivery!
