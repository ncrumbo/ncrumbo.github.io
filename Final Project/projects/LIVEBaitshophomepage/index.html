Welcome to Howard's Lakeside General Store

Here at Howard’s Lakeside General Store. We have everything any angler needs to fish at Buck Creek. Whether you are a serious tournament angler, panfish enthusiast or catfish trot-liner, you can find all the gear you need at our general store. We offer a full line of rods, reels, line and lures and accessories. We also stock live bait including minnows, shiners, goldfish, chubbs, nightcrawlers and frozen baits.

We are anglers first and we enjoy the sport as much as you do. Our knowledge is based on real world experience. We do not just talk about fishing, we are out fishing the local waters to provide you with in-depth knowledge, helpful advice and fishing tips with all the tackle, gear and accessories you need for a successful fishing experience.

In addition, we perform the following services. We sell fishing and hunting licenses to residential customers only and we have a rod and reel spooling and maintenance service to get you back out on the water as fast as possible.

The store is open seven days a week and we are located within a hop and skip from Buck Creek State Park. The next time you are in the area, stop in and see why Howard’s Lakeside General Store is your one stop shopping for all your fishing needs.
