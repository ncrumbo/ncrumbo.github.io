Transportation

Public transportation in Bali is hard due to its traffic and terrain conditions, but it is possible to travel by taxi or to rent a motorbike.

Weather

Bali is situated near the equator and benefits from a tropical climate, with an average year-round temperature of 86°

Language

Balinese, Indonesian, English

Electric

230 V Type C and F plugs

Currency

Indonesian rupiah

Best Seasons To Visit

Bali is during the island’s dry season. From May to October, you won’t have to worry about rain ruining any outdoor activities, and humidity levels are low all summer.
